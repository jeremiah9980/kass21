About Kassidy

Kassidy Cargill is a rising softball athlete whose game is built on toughness, versatility, speed, and impact. What first looked like a natural catcher profile expanded into something even more dynamic: a multi-position player who can make her presence felt in the field, on the bases, and at the plate.

Across her career, Kassidy has developed into a high-contact hitter with real pressure-player value. She owns a .449 career batting average, 1.224 OPS, 97 career hits, and 74 stolen bases with a 94.87% stolen-base success rate.
